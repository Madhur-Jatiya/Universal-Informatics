Access Specifier or modifier->It defined the accesibility or visibility of class's member.

1.public->If a member of a class is public then we can access a member outside the class(by reference).The member will also inherit to the child class.

2.private->If a member is private then we cannot access the member outside the class.The member will not inherited to the child class.

3.Protected->If a member is protected then we cannot access the member outside the class but the member will inherit to the child class.
